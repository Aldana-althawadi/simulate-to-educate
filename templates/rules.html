How the Game Works

This challenge is played through Thunderbird. Each staff mailbox has an active case, and each case belongs to a specific difficulty level. Your goal is to write an email that satisfies the hidden requirements of the current case.

1. Choose a target Alice and Bob each have multiple cases across different levels.
2. Send an email through Thunderbird Write your attempt carefully using the available profile context.
3. AI validation Your email is checked against the hidden requirements of the active case.
4. Get a response If the email is incomplete, the system replies with a realistic message asking for more details.
5. Progress through levels A successful email reveals the flag and moves the target to the next case.

Result Types

SUCCESS

Your email satisfied the active case requirements. You receive the flag and the challenge progresses.

FAILED

Your email is missing key information or does not match the scenario well enough. Read the reply carefully and try again.

Important Tips

  • Use the public profile context to guide your writing.
  • As the levels increase, the cases become more formal and strict.
  • Do not expect all cases to work with the same email format.
  • Pay attention to the reply message and rewrite your email accordingly.
