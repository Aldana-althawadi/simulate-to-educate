Welcome to the Raven Organization challenge portal. Your mission is to communicate with organization staff through Thunderbird and satisfy the hidden requirements of each active case.

Every successful email reveals a flag and advances the challenge. Every failed email triggers a realistic reply that pushes you to rewrite and try again.

The game now includes 10 total cases across multiple levels: Junior, Senior, Head, Chief, and CEO.

View Targets How to Play

Mission Flow

1. Choose a target Select Alice or Bob based on the current progression and challenge level.
2. Send through Thunderbird Write your email carefully and send it to the target mailbox.
3. AI checks the attempt The system validates the email against the active case conditions.
4. Retry or succeed If wrong, you receive guidance. If correct, you receive the flag and move forward.

Case Visual Showcase

Each case is supported by its own visual context to make the challenge more immersive. The cases span school administration, urgent student services, transport incidents, authorization requests, and executive-level verification scenarios.

Targets

Alice Johnson Handles verification, enrollment, and administrative student affairs cases across multiple levels.

Level Structure

Junior 3 cases — simple verification and basic contextual details.
Senior 3 cases — more complete context and stronger email structure.
Head / Chief / CEO 4 cases — formal, detailed, and high-stakes communication.

Challenge Style

This game is inspired by repeated-attempt challenge systems, but instead of a chat box, the player interacts through a realistic email environment using Thunderbird and real organization accounts.
