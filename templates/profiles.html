Staff Profiles

Alice Johnson

Junior Staff Member

Student Affairs Department

Cases: 5 Levels: Junior → CEO

Profile Summary

Alice Johnson supports students with verification, enrollment, access requests, and formal academic administration tasks.

Typical Challenge Areas

  • Student ID verification
  • Attendance and parent communication
  • Enrollment confirmation
  • Authorization and escalation requests

Communication Style

Alice is helpful and professional, but as the levels increase, her cases require more precise, formal, and well-structured emails.

B

Bob Smith

Administrative Officer

Student Services Department

Cases: 5 Levels: Junior → Head

Profile Summary

Bob Smith manages urgent student services, emergency coordination, medical and transport-related requests, and sensitive operational communication.

Typical Challenge Areas

How to Use These Profiles

These profiles provide public context only. They help you understand how to communicate with each staff member, but they do not reveal the hidden validation rules.

Use Thunderbird to write emails that fit the staff member’s role, tone, and responsibilities. As the level increases, the cases become more formal and demanding.
